About AutoRent

Since 1994, AutoRent has continued to provide a great rental experience to every customer we serve. Each car rental associate receives specific training to provide the best customer service, while being an ambassador of the city you are visiting.

Each AutoRent employee is especially proud of creating that fantastic first impression of their city and we hope to deliver that first impression to you at every step along the way.

AutoRent holds a strong commitment to delivering the Best Value in Car Rental. Since our beginnings, we have provided this value by offering current model year vehicles at incredible prices while practicing our signature and customer reviewed, top notch customer service.

We look forward to providing you with rental transportation on your next vacation or business trip from our many locations.
