About Restaurant

The restaurant, since the very first day, was a gathering place for Vilnius bohema and intellectuals – teachers, doctors, actors. Vine&Salad has saved its style and spirit through many years, therefore it atracts both old visitors and modern youth. The restaurant surprises with new ideas, high quality food, good service and original classical surroundings.

Professional staff of the restaurant carefully creates a menu for every season and prepares every meal you order with the utmost care. The restaurant offers elaborate gourmet meals inspired by the culinary art innovations and made by our chefs.

The restaurant’s interior is designed for your comfort and cosiness. A spacious, light, and modern environment allows you to relax and leisurely enjoy quality food.
